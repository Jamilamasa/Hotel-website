The History Of Our Hotel

Lujo hotel has roots dating back to the day of Nigeria's independence in 1960. Designed by Amasa Jamil, it was the world's first designer hotel. The hotel was initially known as Jays place, which contributed handsomly to the neighbourhood, the hotel swiftly becaue a center of attraction and a landmark in the neighbourhood.
