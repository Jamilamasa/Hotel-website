About Lujo Hotel

The Spanish word Lujo meaning "luxury" tells alot about our hotel, Lujo hotel is a luxurious hotel. It is commonly referred to as home away from home, as we have welcoming staff and the best room service to give you the comfort you desire.

Lujo hotel is also known for it's heightened interest in not just the wellbeing of it's customers but the society at large, feeding about 100 kids per week, and giving shelter to the less priviledge. Here at Lujo we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to taste luxury.
